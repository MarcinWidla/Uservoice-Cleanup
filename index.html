⏰ Outdated Ideas

Ideas that are old or have been inactive for an extended period. These are strong candidates for cleanup, archival, or re-evaluation.

Definition: An idea is "outdated" if it meets ANY of these criteria:
  • Created before 2024 (2023 or earlier)
  • Inactive for 180+ days (no updates in the last 6 months)

Action Recommendation: Review for archival if there's no engagement (zero votes, zero comments, no Jira link). High-engagement outdated ideas may warrant PM review to determine if they're still relevant.

⭐ High Priority Ideas

Ideas with significant customer engagement, high revenue impact, or substantial discussion. These represent your most important customer feedback and should be prioritized for product team review.

Definition: An idea is "high priority" if it meets ANY of these criteria:
  • 10+ votes from customers
  • $100,000+ in revenue attached to the requesting accounts
  • 5+ comments indicating active discussion

Action Recommendation: These ideas should have Jira tickets created (if not already linked) and be assigned to product area owners for evaluation and roadmap consideration.

⚠️ Status Gap

Ideas with internal tracking (Jira, assigned owner, or labels) BUT no public status. This is the critical gap - you're working on these internally but customers hear nothing.

Definition: Ideas that have ANY internal status indicator:
  • Linked to Jira - Being tracked in your backlog
  • Assigned owner - Someone is responsible
  • Has labels - Categorized/prioritized (Critical, PENT, 5-biz-day SLA, etc.)
BUT have NO public status update for customers

Why This Matters: These represent broken feedback loops - internal work happening but zero customer communication.

Action Recommendation: Map internal status → public status:

  • Has Jira + In Progress → Set public status "Planned"
  • Has label "Critical" → Set public status "Under Review"
  • Has label "QA" → Set public status "In Progress"
  • Is closed → Set public status "Completed"

Quick Win: These 10,722 ideas can be bulk-updated to improve customer communication!

🔗 No Jira Link

Ideas that have not been connected to your product development tracking system. This indicates these ideas haven't been formally triaged, evaluated, or added to your product backlog.

Definition: Ideas with an empty "Linked Jira Issues" field

Why This Matters: Without Jira linkage, there's no visibility into whether these ideas are being considered, worked on, or closed. This represents a gap in your feedback loop.

Action Recommendation:

  • High-value ideas (with votes/revenue): Create Jira tickets and assign to PMs
  • Low-value ideas (no engagement): Consider archiving with explanation
  • Recent ideas (2025-2026): Route through your intake process

📝 No Public Status

Ideas that lack a customer-facing status update. This means customers who submitted or voted on these ideas have received no communication about whether their feedback is under review, planned, in progress, or completed.

Definition: Ideas with an empty "Public Status Name" field (no status like "Under Review", "Planned", "Completed", etc.)

Why This Matters: Lack of status updates creates a poor customer experience and breaks the feedback loop. Customers don't know if their input was heard or acted upon.

Action Recommendation:

  • Ideas with Jira links: Sync status from Jira → UserVoice (quick win!)
  • Ideas under evaluation: Set status to "Under Review"
  • Ideas being archived: Set status to "Declined" or "Archived" with explanation
  • Completed ideas: Set status to "Completed" and link to release notes

Note: Currently 100% of ideas (12,186) have no public status - this is your biggest opportunity to improve customer communication.
